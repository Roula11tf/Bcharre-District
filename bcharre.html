BCHARRE:



Bcharreh (بشرّي) is a Lebanese local authority which is located in Bcharreh District (Qada'a),
an administrative division of North Lebanon Governorate (Mohafazah).
The municipality is member of Federation of Bcharreh District Municipalities.
