BAZAOUN:




Bazaoun (بزعون) is a Lebanese local authority which is located in Bcharreh District (Qada'a),
an administrative division of North Lebanon Governorate (Mohafazah).
The municipality is member of Federation of Bcharreh District Municipalities.

Location:

  • Distance from Beirut: 107km
  • Altitude: 1,390m
  • Surface (ha): 227
